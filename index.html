  Interests

Computer Vision
I am working with my advisor on high frequency camera pose estimation and learning about solving minimal problems in computer vision using Gröbner Basis.

Programming
I know C++, C, MATLAB, and LabVIEW plus some experience with the assembly languages x86-64, ARM7TDMI, and z80. Some of my projects include two video game console emulators developed in C++ around 2010 and 2013. I am interested in learning C#, Java, Python, and Unity.

Electronics
FPGAs, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, microcontrollers (PIC16F84), NI DAQ, and NI Smart Camera.

RTL Hardware Design
I have experience with Spartan-3A, Zynq-7000 (zc702), and Zynq UltraScale+ (zcu102) devices and Xilinx ISE, Vivado, and Vivado HLS software. I mainly use VHDL and Vivado IP integrator for RTL design, C++ for High Level Synthesis, and C for driver and application development. Some of my projects include AXI4 Video Processing and DMA IPs. I am interested in learning Linux Development for Zynq UltraScale+ devices.

Web Development
I had my own OT server for some time around 2007 and learned some HTML, SQL, PHP, and LUA scripting. It was fun!
